This plugin provides Perl5 & Pod support. Basic functionality may be extended with additional plugins:

  • Languages and Frameworks:
    • Mojolicious
    • HTML::Mason
    • Mason2
    • Template Toolkit 2
    • Embedded Perl
  • Transport Extensions:
    • Docker Support
    • WSL Support
More information may be found in our Wiki.
